What is Microsoft Imagine

Microsoft Imagine (previously known as Dreamspark) connects students with the tools, resources and experiences they need to elevate their skills for today’s working world. Whether it’s building a game, designing an app or launching a project, Microsoft Imagine helps students develop their ideas and boldly bring them to life.
